
Aviation Hull & Liability 

Summary of Cover	
Aviation Hull & Liability Insurance provides indemnity against loss of or damage/destruction to the aircraft and legal liability to third parties and passenger liability resulting from the use of the aircraft. Cover to include War Risks.
