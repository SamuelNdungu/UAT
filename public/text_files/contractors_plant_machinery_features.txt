Extensions / Endorsements
       | - Strike, Riot & Civil Commotion
       | - Overtime, Night Work, Express Freight and Airfreight
       | - Underground Machinery and Equipment
       | - Inland Transit

       | - Water Well Drilling Rigs and Equipment 

Exclusions	

       | - War, invasion and the like
       | - Radioactive contamination
       | - Any consequential loss or liability
       | - Mechanical or electrical breakdown
       | - Exchangeable parts or attachments
       | - Explosion of boilers and other pressure vessels
       | - Vehicles licensed for road use, waterborne vessels or craft 
       | - Total or partial immersion in tidal waters
       | - Transit risks unless specifically endorsed
       | - Wear, tear, depreciation etc.
       | - Damage whilst insured item is undergoing tests
       | - Plant or equipment working underground unless specifically endorsed
       | - Strike riot or civil commotion unless specifically endorsed
       | - Any pre-existing faults or defects
       | - Willful acts or negligence
       | - Manufacturer’s or supplier’s responsibility
       | - Losses discovered at time of taking inventory.
