

Goods in Transit Insurance

Provides cover for loss or damage to various types of goods caused by fire, theft or accidental means while in transit by road, rail or any inland waterway within the geographical area set out in the policy. Cover extends to include temporary storage or housing of the vehicle in the ordinary course of transit.
