

Employee Benefits Insurances

Key Man Insurance
This covers the key person / employee who are vital to the continued growth or profitability of a business, often CEO and/or owner of a company. The insurance offers business protection plan that assists with continuity and preservation of value in the event of an unexpected death or permanent and total disablement or critical illness of the key person /employee.
