

Group Personal Accident – Aviation

Summary of Cover	
Compensation for death or disablement resulting from accidental bodily injury sustained by the insured’s employees as well as crew arising out of the use of the aircraft at any time during the period of insurance.

Persons Insured	
Pilots and crew members
