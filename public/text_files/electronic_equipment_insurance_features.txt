
ELECTRONIC EQUIPMENT INSURANCE

The policy Covers:

Property damage:
Loss or damage to electronic equipment due to fire, theft, electrical or accidental physical damage or any other sudden and unforeseen damage to the property not excluded under the policy.

Reinstatement of data:
Reinstatement of data, and storage media for data (Data media) caused by a defined event referred to section 1.

Increased cost of Working:
The additional expenditure necessarily and reasonably incurred by the Insured following loss or damage to items covered under section 1 above including the cost and expenses incurred in hiring computers and additional staff and overtime payments.
