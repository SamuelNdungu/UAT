
CONTRACTORS ALL RISKS

Summary of Cover	

Indemnity against sudden and unforeseen physical loss of or damage to the property insured and legal liability for accidental bodily injury to or illness of third parties and/or loss of or damage to third party property resulting from any cause not specifically excluded.

Some Important Policy Exclusions

       | - Cessation of work – whether partial or total
       | - Consequential loss of any kind
       | - Loss or damage due to defective design
       | - Cost of repairing, replacing or rectifying defective material and/or workmanship 
       | - Wear, tear, corrosion, oxidation, deterioration due to lack of use and normal atmospheric conditions
       | - Mechanical or electrical breakdown or derangement
       | - Motor Vehicles, water borne vessels or aircraft
       | - Loss of or damage to files, drawings and other similar documents
       | - Loss or damage discovered at time of taking inventory
       | - Liability for damage or injury due vibration or removal or weakening of support
       | - Injury or illness of employees of the contractor or principal
       | - Liability for loss of or damage to property belonging to or in the custody of the Contractor or the Principal
