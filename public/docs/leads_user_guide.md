# Leads Module User Guide

## Overview
The Leads Module is designed to help you manage and track potential customers through your sales pipeline. This guide will walk you through all the features and functionalities available in the leads management system.

## Key Features
- Lead creation for both individual and corporate prospects
- Lead status tracking and management
- Document attachment capabilities
- Lead conversion to customer
- Lead performance metrics
- Export functionality (PDF and Excel)

## Creating a New Lead

### To create a new lead:
1. Navigate to the Leads section
2. Click on the "Create New Lead" button
3. Fill in the required information:

#### Required Fields:
- **Lead Type**: Choose between Individual or Corporate
- **Contact Information**:
  - For Individual: First Name, Last Name
  - For Corporate: Corporate Name
  - Mobile Number
  - Email Address
- **Policy Type**: Select the insurance policy type
- **Lead Source**: Specify how the lead was acquired
- **Follow-up Date**: Schedule the next follow-up
- **Deal Information**:
  - Deal Size: Estimated value of the potential business
  - Probability: Likelihood of conversion (0-100%)
  - Deal Stage: Current stage in the sales process
  - Deal Status: Current status (New, In Progress, Won, Lost)
  - Date Initiated: When the lead was first contacted
  - Closing Date: Expected closure date
- **Next Action**: Planned next step
- **Notes**: Additional information about the lead

### Optional:
- **Documents**: Attach relevant files (max 10MB per file)

## Managing Leads

### Lead Dashboard
The dashboard provides an overview of:
- Total number of leads
- Active leads
- Converted leads
- High-probability leads (>70%)
- Follow-up required leads
- Lost leads

### Filtering Leads
You can filter leads by:
- Active leads
- Converted leads
- High probability leads
- Follow-up required
- Lost leads

### Lead Details
View detailed information about each lead including:
- Contact information
- Deal progress
- Attached documents
- Notes and follow-up dates

## Updating Lead Information
1. Click on the lead you want to update
2. Click the "Edit" button
3. Update the necessary fields
4. Save your changes

## Converting Leads
When a lead is won:
1. Update the deal status to "Won"
2. The system automatically creates a customer record
3. A unique customer code is generated (format: CUS-XXXXX)

## Exporting Lead Data

### Export Options:
1. **PDF Export**
   - Click the "Export" button
   - Select "PDF" format
   - The system will generate a downloadable PDF report

2. **Excel Export**
   - Click the "Export" button
   - Select "Excel" format
   - The system will generate a downloadable Excel file

## Best Practices
1. **Regular Updates**: Keep lead information current
2. **Complete Information**: Fill in all relevant fields
3. **Document Management**: Attach all relevant documents
4. **Follow-up Tracking**: Maintain regular follow-up schedules
5. **Status Updates**: Keep deal status and probability updated

## Tips for Effective Lead Management
- Regularly review and update lead status
- Document all interactions in the notes section
- Set realistic probability percentages
- Keep follow-up dates current
- Attach relevant documents promptly
